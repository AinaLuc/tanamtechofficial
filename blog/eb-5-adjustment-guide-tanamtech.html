EB-5 Visa Adjustment of Status Guide by Tanamtech

Understanding the pathway to U.S. residency through the EB-5 Visa program is crucial. Tanamtech provides an in-depth guide to adjusting your status, highlighting how investors can transition to residency without leaving the U.S.

Overview of Adjustment of Status

Adjustment of status in the EB-5 visa context refers to the process through which investors change their visa status from non-immigrant to conditional permanent resident while in the United States, avoiding the need to return to their home country for visa reissuance.

Eligibility Criteria for Adjustment

To be eligible for an adjustment of status under the EB-5 program, investors must have an approved I-526 petition and ensure that a visa is immediately available. They must also be in the U.S. under a lawful, non-immigrant status.

The Adjustment Process

The adjustment process involves filing Form I-485 with USCIS. Tanamtech assists clients through each step, ensuring clarity in documentation and timeliness in submission, paving the way for a successful transition to conditional permanent residency.
